# Product Mission — Metal_AI

## Mission Statement

Metal_AI is an AI-powered RFQ and cost estimation operating system for metal manufacturing companies.

The platform is designed to transform fragmented inbound commercial/technical requests into structured, auditable, and decision-ready outputs that accelerate quotation cycles and improve profitability.

## End-to-End Transformation Goal

The system should help users move from:

- customer email,
- RFQ text,
- PDF drawing,
- DXF/STEP/JPG/PNG attachments,
- incomplete technical descriptions,
- quantity and material information,

to:

- structured RFQ data,
- detected missing information,
- risk analysis,
- suggested manufacturing route,
- preliminary cost breakdown,
- quote draft,
- internal estimator notes,
- customer clarification questions,
- historical learning from accepted/rejected calculations.

## Scope of Manufacturing Capabilities (Target State)

Metal_AI is intended to support multi-process manufacturing environments, including:

- laser cutting,
- profile cutting,
- bending,
- welding,
- CNC machining,
- drilling,
- deburring,
- painting,
- powder coating,
- galvanizing,
- assembly,
- packaging,
- subcontracting,
- multi-part assemblies.

## Enterprise Costing Intelligence Principles

The platform architecture and product roadmap should preserve and improve:

1. **Company-specific rates** — labor, machine, tooling, setup, overhead.
2. **Company-specific markup rules** — margin strategies by customer/process/risk class.
3. **Estimator feedback loops** — calibration based on won/lost and actual-vs-quoted deltas.
4. **Audit trail and traceability** — explainable decisions and historical accountability.

## Product Non-Goals

Metal_AI must not become:

- a toy chatbot,
- a generic AI wrapper,
- a collection of unrelated features without domain coherence.
